Ganapati Shopee - Your Reliable Electric Device Repairing Service

Welcome to Ganapati Shopee, your one-stop-shop for all your electric device repairing needs. With a team of expert technicians and a customer-centric approach, we are committed to delivering top-notch repairs for smartphones, laptops, household appliances, and more. Trust us to get your devices up and running efficiently and affordably. Contact us today for reliable and prompt service!

Conditions

Once we have successfully repaired your device, we will notify you immediately. You are required to collect the device from our store within 1 months from the date of repair completion.

Notification

We will make reasonable efforts to inform you through the contact details provided during the repair intake process (phone number, email, etc.) once your device is ready for collection.

Security

At Ganapati Shopee, we prioritize data security, ensuring your personal information remains safe and in the right format throughout the repair process.

Repairing Info

To maintain transparency, we provide a video of the repair before you make any payment, allowing you to verify the quality of our work.
